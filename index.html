WHAT IS IO-CON?

A PREMIUM COMIC & ART EXPERIENCE

🎨

ARTIST-CENTRIC

IO-CON values both creators and collectors. Due to the curated nature of the convention, we will be hand-selecting artists for both cohesion and authenticity.

⭐

PREMIUM EXPERIENCE

An intimate event for those who want one-on-one interactions and to support their favorite (or soon-to-be!) artist. Quality time and products over quantity.

🛡️

NO AI ART

We strictly enforce a human-art-only policy. Every piece sold at IO-CON is verified authentic—protecting the integrity of human creativity in the age of AI.

💜

COMMUNITY FIRST

We will be holding a toy and book drive for the Happy Wheels Program, a South Carolina nonprofit that brings comfort and joy to children receiving medical care by delivering brand-new toys, games, books, and activities to pediatric patients year-round. Through weekly visits to children's hospitals across the state, Happy Wheels helps brighten hospital stays, and we're proud to be part of that effort. Click here for gift guidelines and their active wishlist.

SECURE YOUR TABLE TODAY

Join our curated selection of artists at IO-CON's inaugural event. Only 10 Artist Alley spots available to maintain our premium, intimate atmosphere. We carefully curate artwork to align with the convention's theme—ensuring a cohesive experience that benefits both artists and attendees. All artwork must be human-created—no AI art permitted.

EXHIBITOR TERMS & CONDITIONS

Table Space & Setup: Artist Alley includes a 6ft table, 2 chairs, and 2 exhibitor badges. Setup is Friday 4:00–7:00 PM. Teardown is Sunday 3:00–5:00 PM — no early breakdown. Exhibitors must remain for the full duration of both days.

Payment: Full payment ($100) due upon acceptance via TicketLeap.

Refunds: 45+ days before event: full refund minus $25 fee. 21-44 days: 50% refund. Less than 21 days: no refund. If IO-CON cancels, full refunds issued.

Content & Conduct: All artwork must be human-created — no AI art. X-rated material subject to approval; contact us before applying. Professional conduct required at all times.

Sales & Taxes: Exhibitors collect/remit their own sales tax. IO-CON takes no commission. Bring your own payment processing.

Liability: IO-CON is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged items. Exhibitors encouraged to carry insurance.

Curated Event: IO-CON is a curated show — application does not guarantee acceptance.
